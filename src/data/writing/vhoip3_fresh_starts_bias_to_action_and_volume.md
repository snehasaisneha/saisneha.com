## Fresh Starts

I have spent most of my life being in favour of fresh starts. I mean, why not, right? You screw something up, just start fresh and try again. As a teenager and young adult, I have always found it particularly attractive to do that. Lately though, I've been realising that it doesn't quite work for me. And perhaps, to some degree, it never did.

The rise of digital tools has made it far too simple to get fresh starts. You don't like your writing? Cmd (or Ctrl) + A, Backspace/Delete, and start fresh. Even better, just delete the file and try to write it anew.

I recently realised that this habit had trickled into my attempts at self development.
